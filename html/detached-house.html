DESCRIPTION

Akimbo’s bold exterior will define the Brentwood skyline. The 40-storey project’s architectural definition is marked by its angled, offset balconies that wrap the height of the building, providing both sun shade and unobstructed views. For the first time ever, an architectural landmark building designed to the world-class level of the most iconic buildings in Downtown Vancouver, comes to Brentwood.
